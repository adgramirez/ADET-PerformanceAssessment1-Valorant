Name: Erik Torsten

Country: Sweden

Class: Human

// BIOGRAPHY

Breach, the bionic Swede, fires powerful, targeted kinetic blasts to aggressively clear a path through enemy ground. The damage and disruption he inflicts ensures no fight is ever fair.

Breach is a congenital amputee with artificial arms built by himself, with Raze implementing some major upgrades and custom work. Breach has implied his previous life was as a mercenary.

If there's anyone who knows the risk - and the thrill - of their job as a VALORANT agent best, it's probably Breach. This bionic man has hinted at having a long history of being in the battlefield, and any shootout is always fun for him.
