Country: China

// BIOGRAPHY

The stronghold of China, Sage creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes. Able to revive fallen friends and stave off aggressive pushes, she provides a calm center to a hellish fight.

Originating from the Shaanxi province in China, Sage is a Radiant healer whose capacity to turn the tide of battle is immeasurable.

Sage has a kind and compassionate personality, considered by some to be almost 'mother-like'. While still relatively young, Sage exudes an inner wisdom of someone much older and more mature. She genuinely cares about the wellbeing of her allies, and places great emphasis on collaboration and team victories over personal glory.
