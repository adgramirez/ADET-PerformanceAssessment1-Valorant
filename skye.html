// PERSONAL PROFILE

Name: Kirra Foster

Country: Australia

Class: Radiant

// BIOGRAPHY

Hailing from Australia, Skye and her band of beasts trail-blaze the way through hostile territory. With her creations hampering the enemy, and her power to heal others, the team is strongest and safest by Skye's side.

Skye is a Radiant from Eastern Australia, defender of her homeland's flora and fauna. She uses her Radiant powers to animate wooden figures which she calls her pack. The pack is her company, as she usually works alone.

While Skye has said that she was not used to working with other people prior to joining VALORANT, she has quickly shown herself to be a team player through and through, guiding and reminding her team to move as one unit and work together as they complete their mission. With her affinity to feel and sense the natural world and everything else around her, Skye is naturally observant, and will quickly note whenever she senses something unusual with her surroundings.
